Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2023. Our commitment to your privacy is as pure as our cocoa. We believe in being honest and direct about how we handle your information.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect the information necessary to provide you with the finest chocolate experience. This includes:

  • Contact details like your name, email address, and phone number.
  • Delivery information such as your physical shipping address.
  • Payment details (processed securely via our partners, we never store your full card number).
  • Your chocolate preferences and order history.

2. How We Use It

Your information helps us craft a better experience for you. We use your data to:

  • Process and deliver your gourmet chocolate orders.
  • Communicate important updates about your delivery.
  • Send you occasional treats, like special offers (if you've opted in).
  • Improve our website functionality and chocolate range based on popular demand.

3. Your Rights

You are in control of your data. At any time, you have the right to:

  • Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Ask us to correct any inaccuracies in your records.
  • Request that we delete your personal information from our systems.
  • Withdraw your consent for marketing communications with a single click.

4. Data Security

We treat your data with the same care we give our chocolates. We use industry-standard encryption and security protocols to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. Our systems are regularly monitored for potential vulnerabilities.

5. Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses small files called "cookies" to remember your preferences and keep track of your shopping cart. These help us understand which parts of our site are most popular, allowing us to refine the digital browsing experience just as we refine our recipes.

6. Sharing with Third Parties

We never sell your personal information to third parties. We only share data with essential service partners who help us function, such as delivery couriers and payment processors. These partners are strictly required to keep your data confidential and secure.

7. Children's Privacy

While our chocolates are enjoyed by all ages, our website is intended for adults aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with information, please contact us immediately.

8. International Transfers

As a global brand, your data may occasionally be processed in countries outside of your own. When this happens, we ensure that the same high standards of data protection and security are applied, regardless of the geographic location.

9. Policy Changes

Like our seasonal collections, this policy may evolve. Any significant changes will be posted here and, if substantial, communicated to you via email. We encourage you to review this page periodically to stay informed about how we protect your privacy.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this policy, or if you'd like to exercise your data rights, our privacy team is here to help.
